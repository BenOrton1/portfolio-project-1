Comics

Where no one goes

A series of short stories about the difficulties of finding ones own way in life. You can read Vol. 1 WHERE NO ONE GOES on Webtoons and purchase Vol.1 as well as Vol. 2 WHERE NO ONE LIVES in my Shop.

INSIDE PHOBIA

A coming of age, mystery webcomic about the teenage girls Susan, Brighid and Alma. On their quest to survive the attacks of a bodysnatching villain they have to learn how to deal with loss, fear and love.

Inside Phobia updates a page every monday and is an affiliated Hiveworks project that can be read on insidephobia.com.

THE SECRET OF tÙ

A adventure story set in a fantasy china. To save their cousin Bo from dying, Jiang and Hien are on a mission to find a magical creature that is said to have the cure to every illness. When Jiang and Hiens uncle Lord Gongson passes away, their quest becomes a race for their own lifes, as their aunt Lady Gongsun will punish their failure with death.
